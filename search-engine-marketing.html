What is Search Engine Marketing ?

Search Engine Marketing (SEM) is a digital marketing strategy to increase a website’s exposure in search engine results pages (SERPs). It is a type of Paid Advertising, where businesses or an individual pays only for those keywords or search terms which is relevant to their products or services. It also helps to reach a wider audience and attract more users to our website.

Whenever a user searches a search term according to his need, then ads of different businesses related to that search term, may appear at the top or bottom of the Search Engine Result Pages. And, above all, advertisers only have to pay for a click whenever a user clicks on that particular ad, which is popularly known as PPC (Pay-Per-Click).

With Search Engine Marketing Agency in Mohali, you can explore the full potential of the Paid Advertising. We will assist you to increase your business’s online visibility and attract more customers. Also, we will help you to increase organic traffic on your website by using our SEO service.

How does Search Engine Marketing ?

As we know, these days, a number of websites are built for a number of different products or services to provide information. A large number of website and webpages are built on a regular basis and search engines are continuously crawl & index those pages. All those web pages are relevant to a given search term. Search Engine, further crawl and validate those web pages with some well-defined algorithms.

After that, search engine indexes and assigns a ranking to web pages on the basis of their user experience, authority and content quality. Search Engine Result Pages shows the most suitable web pages links. In order to provide the most relevant and accurate results for their searches for their users.
